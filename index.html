Hi, we're Pydra

We're a small organization comprised of 3 teenagers who are trying to change the IT landscape by providing free compute power to students and non-profits with "microservers".

Our mission is to empower the next generation by providing free, accessible compute power to students and non-profits.

Welcome to The Pydra Project
What's a microserver?
A microserver is a small, power efficient computer that can be remotely accessed to be used for learning or aiding in shared goals.
