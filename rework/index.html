Who are we?

We're a nonprofit providing students with a cost effective equivalent of a virtual private server, aka a microserver :D

What am I getting when using a microserver?

The hardware we use for microservers are mainly Raspberry Pi's, so they can be used as any other thing you want them to! Just SSH and you're good to go!
