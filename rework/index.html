What is The Pydra Project?

The Pydra Project is an organiztion dedicated to providing microservers to students and nonprofits alike.

What's a microserver?

A microserver is a generalized, small, power efficient computer that can be remotely accessed to be used for learning or aiding in shared goals such as selfhosting a program.
